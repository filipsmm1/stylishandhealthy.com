What's inside

Everything you need to
grow beautifully.

The 279 Day Journal isn't just a diary. It's a structured self-improvement system wrapped in the softest, most intentional design — built for girls who want real progress that feels good.

01
Daily Pages

Each of 279 days has its own spread: a morning intention, an evening reflection, a gratitude line, and a one-line habit tracker. Simple. Consistent. Beautiful.

02
Habit Tracker

A monthly visual habit grid so you can see your progress at a glance. Track up to 10 habits — skincare, water, movement, sleep, journaling, or whatever your goals are.

03
Wellness Check-ins

Weekly mini-reviews where you rate your mood, sleep, hydration, and self-care. Gentle accountability, not pressure — just a quiet check-in with yourself.

04
Guided Prompts

279 unique prompts — no repeats. Ranging from light reflection ("what made me smile today") to deeper thinking ("what belief is holding me back this month").

05
Monthly Reviews

At the end of each month: a two-page spread to review wins, lessons, what you want more of, and intentions for the next month. Growth you can actually see.

06
Designed to feel good

Warm cream pages, elegant typography, soft pink accents. The 279 Day Journal is designed to be something you want to open — not something that feels like homework.
