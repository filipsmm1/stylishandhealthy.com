Privacy Policy.

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: 2026

1. What we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services. This may include:

  • Your email address when you sign up for our newsletter or free downloads
  • Your name when provided voluntarily
  • Payment information when making purchases (processed securely by our payment provider -- we do not store card details)
  • Basic usage data through analytics tools (pages visited, time on site, device type)

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Send you the newsletter or content you signed up for
  • Deliver digital products you have purchased or requested
  • Process transactions
  • Improve the website and understand how it is used
  • Respond to your messages and enquiries

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

3. Email communications

If you sign up for our newsletter, you will receive regular emails from us. Every email includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can remove yourself from our list at any time, and we will process that request promptly.

4. Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to gather anonymous analytics data. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the site.

5. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to operate this website, including email marketing platforms, analytics tools, and payment processors. These providers have their own privacy policies and may process your data in accordance with them.

6. Data retention

We retain your email address and name for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at contact@stylishandhealthy.com.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing or request its restriction. To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@stylishandhealthy.com.
